About Us

The people behind the project.

This project started out as a class assignment for the course Transmedia Skills & Craft, a seminar taught by Lori Grinker and the TA Trevor Pascal at the New School. Over the semester of Spring 2021 the students Summer Safi, Fatima Coulibaly, Lillian Penther, and Ashley Sayarath worked on this assignment. We wanted to educate people about climate change through different mediums whether it's based on textual or visual elements. If you want to learn more details about this assignment you can submit this form and we'll respond as soon as we can.
